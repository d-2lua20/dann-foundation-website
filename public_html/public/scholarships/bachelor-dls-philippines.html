De La Salle–College of Saint Benilde (DLS-CSB) Scholarships 2026

The De La Salle–College of Saint Benilde offers various scholarship opportunities for deserving students to pursue undergraduate programs in the Philippines. The scholarships aim to develop future leaders with academic excellence and compassion for others.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Open to local and international students.
  • Excellent academic standing in previous education.
  • Active involvement in community or leadership activities.

Benefits

  • Full or partial tuition fee coverage.
  • Access to student development programs and mentoring.
