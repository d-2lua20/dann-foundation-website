University Japan ADB Scholarship 2026 (Masters)

The Asian Development Bank–Japan Scholarship Program (ADB–JSP) provides full scholarships for citizens of ADB member countries to pursue postgraduate studies in economics, science, management, technology, and related fields at participating universities in Japan.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a citizen of an ADB borrowing member country.
  • Hold a Bachelor’s degree or equivalent with superior academic record.
  • At least two years of professional experience after graduation.
  • Not older than 35 years at the time of application.

Benefits

  • Full tuition fees.
  • Monthly allowance and housing.
  • Medical and travel expenses.
