University of Auckland Doctoral Scholarships 2026

The University of Auckland Doctoral Scholarships are prestigious awards offered to highly motivated students pursuing PhD studies in New Zealand. These scholarships aim to support research excellence and innovation across diverse academic fields.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Open to both domestic and international students.
  • Applicants must have a Master’s degree with strong academic performance.
  • Demonstrated research experience and a clear research proposal.
  • English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL) meeting the University’s requirements.

Benefits

  • Full tuition fee coverage for the entire program duration.
  • Annual living stipend of NZD 28,000.
  • Health insurance and research support grants.
  • Access to top-tier facilities and supervision.

Application Deadline

July 1, 2026

How to Apply

Applicants must submit an online application via the official University of Auckland portal, including academic transcripts, a research proposal, recommendation letters, and proof of English proficiency.
